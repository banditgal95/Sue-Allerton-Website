I started writing when I was a child, I loved transporting myself to new worlds,
and being the only one able to create the rules there. I guess that's what all
children want; to be able to create their own rules.

My Chosen genre, over all else; has to be fantasy. Purely for the reason that
I get to create my own worlds, rules, species. I can make the sky purple and
it wouldn't matter because it's my world.

It did take me a while to get into my stride. I have the attention span of my
dog...which, trust me; doesn't last long. I found I was getting bored of my own
writing, which made me think; if I'm bored just writing this, who is actually
going to want to read this?

It wasn't until my late teens when I actually wrote a book from start to finish without
getting bored once. I was so proud of myself just for that alone.
After that it was pretty straight forward, I made a cover and published with Amazon Kindle.
I didn't feel like the traditional publishing route was for me, and in all honesty;
I didn't even try to get published by a publishing house. I enjoy the freedom of
self-publishing and it's so easy to do.
