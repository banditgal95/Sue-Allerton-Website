I came up with the idea for the 'A Tale Of The Gods' series because I love
Greek mythology and I wanted to give it a modern spin while sticking with
the characters that we know so well.
I decided to follow the story of Eris, The Goddess of Discord.
and a human man named Axle; he was a fun character for me to write,
As well as all the difficulties in his own life, he has to deal with
the realisation that perhaps all the myths and legends are true.

Eris was also fun for me to write with. I can follow so many different paths
with her and essentially change how we percieve certain things about the Myths.

This was the first book I finished completely. I am happy with the result and I
aware of some errors within the text which I am going through and editing.
When I finished writing the book I was so excited to get it out there that I
skipped the editting stage...rookie mistake.

I hope you enjoy following Axle and Eris through this adventure in
book 1: Hell's Rising, and book 2: Descended.

In a world of uncertainty, one thing we can always be sure of is
our thirst for the unknown. Our hope to find just a slither of
fantasy in this dull world is what drives us.
As a young historian, Axel is forever searching for clues into the past;
he craves the truth behind the stories and rumours that have been passed
through time for generations. But when he finds out the answers to his questions,
will he be ready?

The fight continues for Axel as he struggles to come to grips with the world he
lives in. Will he be able to come to terms with his new life or will he give
everything up to go back to the world he once knew, but first; he'll need to figure
out how to get back there.
