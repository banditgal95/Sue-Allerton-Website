So in this page I wanted to keep you updated about what will be coming up.
I am still working on the 'A Tale Of The Gods' series, but I'm yet to find
inspiration for the third installment, I want it to be the best it can be
for you guys and I am getting some good ideas while wanting to explor the
characters in more detail, I think you'll be surprised with some of the twists.
Although whereas I would love to get my main series out quicker, I do have
other ideas I'm currently exploring, both very different which I think will
vary the audience as they will both be suited to very different readers I think.
Both of which I'm very excited for, I hope you'll enjoy reading them as much as I'm
enjoying writing them.

I haven't actually decided on a title for either of these books yet, but this one
I've been calling 'Willow Creek'
I wrote this book because it's something I'm so passionate about, it's something
I've done since I was a kid, other than writing; it's based around horses.
This isn't something I've ever written about before so I am happy with how it's
turning out. It's a different genre for me to explore which I am loving right now.
I've decided to base it on the harshness, and the love; of competitive riding.
The effects that it can have on both horse and rider and how different sports
demand different things. I wanted to focus very much on the bond between horse
and rider.

Again, I haven't decided on the title for this one but I've been calling it
'Underworld'. This one goes back to my favourite genre being fantasy.
It's slightly darker than the 'A Tale Of The Gods' series and again, based on
religion. But more the Christian side of things. I wanted to write about Angels
And Demons. What if we got the stories were true? What if they were wrong?
